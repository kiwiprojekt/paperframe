Paperframe client installation instructions:

  1. Connect your jailbroken e-reader to your computer using a USB cable.
  2. Download paperframe.sh using the button below and copy it directly to the documents directory on your Kindle (typically mounted as /mnt/us/documents/).
  3. Eject the USB connection safely.
  4. Open library page on your kindle and click Paperframe Client book
  5. Your device will request its personalized script, clear and update the E-Ink screen, and then enter a deep CPU sleep cycle until the next scheduled update.
  6. You can force refresh the display at any time by pressing the power button.
  7. To exit the script disable the device in the Paperframe Manager.
